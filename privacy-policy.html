Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Privacy is important to us, especially for children. This Privacy Policy explains how PetPal works and what information is stored when your child uses our app. PetPal is designed for children with parental supervision.

1. Designed for Children with Parental Consent

PetPal is designed to help children learn responsibility through pet care tracking. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Parents or guardians should review this policy and supervise their child's use of the app.

Important: By allowing your child to use PetPal, you consent to the information practices described in this policy.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

PetPal stores all information locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The information stored includes:

  • Pet information your child enters (pet names, types)
  • Tracking data (food, water, exercise schedules)
  • Vet appointment dates
  • Medication reminders
  • Photos of pets (stored only on your device)

3. What We Do NOT Collect

PetPal does NOT collect or transmit:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Any data from children to external servers

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us
  • Data is protected by your device's security features
  • If you delete the app, all data is removed from your device
  • We recommend using device passcodes and security features

5. No Data Sharing

Because we do not collect any personal information, we have nothing to share. Your child's data stays on your device and is never transmitted to PetPal or any third parties.

6. Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review: Review any information your child has entered in the app (all stored on your device)
  • Delete: Delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • Control: Supervise and control your child's use of the app

7. Third-Party Services

PetPal does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app functions entirely on your device without external connections.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage parents to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@petpal.app
  • Support Page: PetPal Support

For Parents: PetPal is committed to protecting children's privacy. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we collect no personal information, your child's privacy is fully protected. If you have any concerns, please contact us at support@petpal.app.
