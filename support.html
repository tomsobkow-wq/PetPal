Support & Help Center

Need help? We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you may have with PetPal. Check out our FAQ below or contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a pet profile?

To create a new pet profile:

  1. Open the PetPal app
  2. Tap the "+" button on the home screen
  3. Select "Add New Pet"
  4. Fill in your pet's information (name, breed, age, etc.)
  5. Add a photo (optional)
  6. Tap "Save" to create the profile

How do I set up reminders?

To set up a reminder for your pet:

  1. Open your pet's profile
  2. Tap "Reminders" or the bell icon
  3. Tap "Add Reminder"
  4. Choose the type (feeding, medication, vet appointment, etc.)
  5. Set the date and time
  6. Choose to make it recurring if needed
  7. Tap "Save"

Can I manage multiple pets?

Yes! PetPal allows you to create and manage profiles for multiple pets. There's no limit to the number of pets you can add to your account.

How do I share a pet profile with family members?

To share a pet profile:

  1. Open the pet's profile
  2. Tap the share icon
  3. Enter the email address of the person you want to share with
  4. Choose their permission level (view only or edit)
  5. Send the invitation

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

How do I export my pet's health records?

To export health records:

  1. Open your pet's profile
  2. Go to "Health Records"
  3. Tap the menu icon (three dots)
  4. Select "Export as PDF"
  5. Choose where to save or share the file

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Open the app settings
  2. Scroll to "Account Management"
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

Does PetPal work offline?

Yes, PetPal offers offline functionality. You can view and edit pet profiles, set reminders, and access stored information without an internet connection. Changes will sync automatically when you're back online.

How do I restore my data on a new device?

Your data is automatically backed up to the cloud. Simply log in with your account credentials on your new device, and all your pet profiles and data will be restored.

What types of reminders can I set?

PetPal supports various reminder types including:

  • Feeding schedules
  • Medication reminders
  • Vet appointments
  • Grooming sessions
  • Vaccination due dates
  • Exercise and playtime
  • Custom reminders
