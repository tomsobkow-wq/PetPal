Support & Help Center

Need help? We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you may have with PetPal. Check out our FAQ below or contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a pet?

To add a new pet:

  1. Open the PetPal app
  2. Tap the "+" or "Add Pet" button
  3. Enter your pet's name and information
  4. Add a photo of your pet (optional)
  5. Save the profile

How do I track food and water?

PetPal lets you manually set and track your pet's feeding and water schedule:

  1. Open your pet's profile
  2. Select "Food" or "Water"
  3. Set how long it takes for your pet to consume
  4. Track throughout the day

Can I track multiple pets?

Yes! PetPal allows you to track unlimited pets. There's no limit to the number of pets you can add.

How do I add vet appointments?

To track vet appointments:

  1. Open your pet's profile
  2. Select "Vet Appointments"
  3. Add the appointment date
  4. Save the appointment

Is my data secure?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT collect or transmit any personal information. Your pet's information stays private and secure on your device. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my data?

To delete all data, simply delete the PetPal app from your device. All information is stored locally, so removing the app will remove all data.

Does PetPal work offline?

Yes! PetPal works completely offline. All data is stored on your device, so you don't need an internet connection to use the app.

Can I use PetPal on multiple devices?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, each device will have its own separate data. There is no cloud syncing between devices.

What can I track in PetPal?

PetPal helps you track:

  • Food consumption schedules
  • Water intake
  • Exercise and playtime
  • Vet appointments
  • Medication reminders
  • Pet photos
